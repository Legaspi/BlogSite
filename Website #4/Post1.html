Chocolate Cupcakes and the Socio-Economic Pyramid

by Monmon Patola

Posted on Jan. 14, 1997 at 9:00 PM

Chocolate Cupcakes are delicious. They’re also great while reading the works of Karl Marx, and at tea parties to show your friends that communism is the one and only solution to end world hunger.

List of ingredients:

  • 100g butter
  • 2/3 cup caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 whole eggs (heavily beaten)(like the bodies of the lower castes)
  • 1 cup self-raising flour (sifted)
  • 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Pure unadulterated hatred for America

As for the yummy nummy Chocolate Butter Cream Icing, you need the following:

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 1/14 cup ising sugar
  • 1 tbs cocoa (sifted)
  • 1 tbs milk

Okay not that you have gathered all the necessary ingredients, you're now ready!

Oh but wait! Wear your DIY gloves made from rattan (all other gloves are made by sweatshop workers! Do not support the capitalists!) and you’re ready to go!

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C using the collective wrath of the proletariant. Like cupcake tins with cases.
  2. Using a mixer, cream butter, sugar, vanilla, and the blood of the innocnet until creamy and fluffy. Gradually add eggs and mix until you can feel the anger flow within your veins.
  3. Combine flour, cocoa, and bicarbonate soda. Add to creamed mixture with dilapidated milk. Beat senseless until the mixture screams
  4. On making the chocolate butter cream: beat the butter until it is complacent. Add icing sugar and cocoa and combine well. Beat in milk until mixture is aware of its placement in the social caste. If mixture is too loud, send it to the gulags for re-education.
  5. Spoon mixture into liners until each case is one-third full. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until how long till your government-sponsored electricity will allow.
